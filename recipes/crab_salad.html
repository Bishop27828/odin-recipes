Crab Salad



Description:

This crab salad recipe, featuring budget-friendly ingredients, is as easy as it gets.

Ingredients:

  • Imitation crab meat: This recipe starts with a pound of imitation crab meat.
  • Celery: Finely chopped celery lends flavor and texture.
  • Dressing and mayonnaise: A combination of mayonnaise and reduced-fat ranch dressing ensures a flavorful, creamy result.
  • Sugar: White sugar adds subtle sweetness.
  • Parsley: Minced fresh parsley gives the crab salad a bright pop of color and flavor.

Steps:

  1. Mix the ingredients until combined.
  2. Chill overnight.
  3. Sit before serving.
